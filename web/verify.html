Verify a certificate

Paste the survivor-cert-….json file the bot posted to your group (or open it and copy the text). Your browser recomputes every pick hash, checks every ed25519 signature, rebuilds the certificate hash, and looks for it in the anchor wallet's Memo trail on Solana devnet. We are not involved.

What "verified" means here

Each pick was committed as sha256(pool | round | player | pick | salt) and signed by the survivor's own key. Round roots were anchored on-chain before outcomes were announced; the certificate hash sits in the same Memo trail. If the checks go green, this exact path of picks existed, signed, before the football happened — and nobody, including us, could have written it afterwards.

devnet keeps ~4 days of transaction history, so we check the anchor account's recent Memo trail; older pools are archived as evidence JSONs in the repo.

everything on this page runs in your browser: WebCrypto for the hashes and signatures, one public RPC call for the Memo trail. view source, it's short.
